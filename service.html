IN MUTABARUKA's HIGH SCHOOL, we have many different services we offer:
  • Facility Management & Maintenance:

    • This includes tasks like cleaning, repairs, upkeep of grounds, and ensuring the school building and equipment are in good working order.

  • Security:

    • Providing a safe and secure environment for students and staff, which may involve security personnel, access control, and emergency protocols.

  • Catering & Food Services:

    • Managing the school cafeteria, preparing and serving meals, and ensuring the availability of healthy food options.

  • Transportation:

    • Arranging for student transportation, including buses and other forms of transport, and ensuring safe and reliable travel.

  • Administrative & Clerical Support:

    • Providing administrative support to teachers and staff, including tasks like filing, record-keeping, and communication.

  • Supply Management:

    • Procuring and managing school supplies, equipment, and materials, ensuring they are available when needed.

  • Technology Support:

    • Providing technical support for computers, software, and other technology used in the school.

  • Student Support Services:

    • This includes services like counseling, health services, and special education support, which are vital for student well-being and success.
